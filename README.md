# RedeemSeal

RedeemSeal is a SaaS platform built to help businesses create, manage, and grow digital loyalty programs.

Our platform allows businesses to offer digital loyalty cards and wallet passes that customers can save directly to Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. RedeemSeal is designed for businesses that want a modern, simple, and mobile-first way to reward repeat customers, improve retention, and manage loyalty campaigns digitally.

## What RedeemSeal does

* Creates digital loyalty cards and wallet passes
* Supports Apple Wallet and Google Wallet integrations
* Helps businesses manage customer loyalty programs
* Enables QR-based customer interactions
* Provides tools for tracking loyalty activity and engagement
* Supports digital-first customer retention strategies

## Project status

RedeemSeal is currently under active development. Our repositories may remain private while we continue building, testing, and preparing the platform for production.
